NOUNS

Anoun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows:

  • Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea or thing.
  • Common: It is the name prossessed by thungs in general.
  • Collective: It is the name representing a group of things
  • Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotoins, condition or actions.

PRONOUN

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronouns are classified as follows:

  • Personal pronoun: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substiute of the noun.
  • Interrogative pronoun: It stands as the answer to the quetion about a person, place or a thing.
  • Indefinite noun: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.
  • Relative pronoun: It realates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.
  • Demonstrative pronoun: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.
  • Reflexive pronoun: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
